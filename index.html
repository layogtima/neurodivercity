We Are NOT a Support Group

What We ARE

  • A fun landing space for neurospicy humans
  • Evidence-based and neurodiversity-affirming
  • A place to find your people and share experiences
  • Celebrating all neurotypes without "fixing" anyone
  • Building community through shared understanding

What We're NOT

  • A therapy or support group
  • A place for trauma dumping
  • Offering professional medical advice
  • A dating platform
  • Tolerating hate or discrimination

Our Philosophy

"Neurodiversity is not about being broken and needing to be fixed. It's about being beautifully different and needing to be understood, supported, and celebrated for the unique perspectives we bring to the world."

Community Guidelines

Respect & Consent

No discussing others' diagnoses without consent. Ask before DMing. Respect boundaries.

Inclusive Language

Use person-first or identity-first language as preferred. No ableist slurs or harmful stereotypes.

Keep It Chill

We're here for good vibes. No drama, no dogpiling, no sealioning. Just humans being cool to humans.
